Instructions

The Sport Aptitude Quiz for Heros Academy consists of three parts: imitate, adapt and transpose part.

The first and second part of quiz consist of 5 questions for each part, and the third part consists of 10 questions.

Quiz Rules

  • 1- It is a One-Way quiz you can't go back to the previous question.

  • 2- You Must answer the question to move forward to the next one.

  • 3-You should know that The Fastest you answer the More Marks you get:

    -if you answer in 10 seconds you will get 20 points
    -if you answer in 20 seconds you will get 15 points
    -if you answer in 30 seconds you will get 10 points
    -if you answer in 40 seconds you will get 5 points
